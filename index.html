About the event

We at IOSD MSIT are very excited to announce HackMSIT 2.0. Hack MSIT presents the opportunity for developers across Delhi to converge and work on exciting real-world problems we have got for them. Our previous event attracted many developers from across a diverse set of framework specializations and languages, who gave their best to develop unique solutions to challenging problems. HackMSIT 2.0 promises to be a hub of creativity and innovation for developing unique software and hardware products. We aim to further nurture the budding coding culture across our country, and HackMSIT 2.0 is a step in that direction. Bring teams of up to 4 people and take a shot at that coveted trophy.

Road to HackMSIT

HackMSIT consist of 3 phases:

  • Phase 1 (8th March, 12:00 AM to 9th March, 10:00 AM | ONLINE): All the registered teams will be notified of the themes.. Teams will pick a theme and submit their idea and plan of action to solve a problem in that particular theme.
  • Phase 2 (9th March 04:00 PM to 12th March to 12:00 AM | ONLINE): All the teams must submit their project implementation/code and a video demonstration of their project. Following these submissions, the list of shortlisted teams will be released on 12th March.
  • Phase 3 (13th March | ONLINE to 14th March | ON VENUE): Selected teams should start working on the product online. They will be mentored for improvements in their projects and will code these changes at the venue (MSIT) on 14th March. Post this, teams will pitch their project, based on which winners of the hackathon will be decided.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get all your answer here

What is a hackathon?
Hackathons are super cool events full of fun, development, technology and start-up atmosphere. Attendees work in teams to create awesome apps, chat-bots, robots or whatever you like!
What does it cost to attend?
HACKMSIT2.0 is free of charge!
What skills do I need to participate?
Any skills! We are not just looking for coders! Whether you are a designer, a programmer or a creative mate, anyone can participate.
What should I bring with me?
Laptop, charger and cool friends. We will provide working spaces, regular meals, drinks and snacks. Feel free to bring extra hardware and gadgets.
What can I expect from HACKMSIT 2.0?
An entire day of great company, good food and plenty of swags!
Do you have a code of conduct?
Yes. Be respectful, harassment and abuse are never tolerated.
Can I register as a team?
Yes, you can! You can also participate as individual. Team size can be 1-4 members.
Are we supposed to use any particular coding language?
No, you can code in any language.
What’s the maximum size of a team?
The team size is required to be no more than 4 people. We also advise to form a team of at least 3 people. However, you can also register without having a team.
Can I bring my existing project to the hackathon?
We apologize, but all hackathon's projects should be developed during the event's timeline from scratch. We want honest conditions for all hackers. That's why all the project should be done during the hackathon's timeline.
What is the criterion of selection?
You will be judged on the quality, innovation, feasibility and impact of your idea/plan of action, project implementation.
I didn’t find the answer to my question!
No problem, just write us at iosdmsit@gmail.com
